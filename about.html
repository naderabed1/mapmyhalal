About MapMyHalal

What is MapMyHalal?

MapMyHalal is a free directory that helps Muslims and halal-conscious consumers across the United States find halal food businesses near them. Whether you're looking for a halal restaurant for dinner, a grocery store to stock up on halal meat, or a local halal farm, MapMyHalal makes it simple.

Just enter your city or ZIP code, select a category, and instantly get a list of halal businesses with addresses, phone numbers, hours, and reviews.

What we cover

🍽️

Restaurants

Halal restaurants of all cuisines — American, Middle Eastern, South Asian, and more.

🛒

Grocery Stores

Halal markets, grocery stores, and butcher shops carrying certified halal products.

🌿

Farms

Zabiha halal farms and halal meat producers across the United States.

How it works

MapMyHalal uses advanced AI technology to search for and compile information about halal businesses across the United States. Enter a location, and our system retrieves relevant listings including contact details, operating hours, and customer feedback.

We are constantly working to improve our coverage. If you notice a listing that needs updating, we appreciate your patience as our database continues to grow.
